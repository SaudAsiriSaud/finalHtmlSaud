- Tabuk University is located in the city of Tabuk, northwest of Saudi Arabia, and was founded in 2006. The university seeks to achieve its mission of “preparing an educated, educated and successful generation capable of serving society and achieving sustainable development.”

- The university has 18 faculties and institutes, offering more than 100 educational programs in various scientific and humanitarian disciplines.

- Tabuk University seeks to achieve its vision of “being a leading university in the field of education and scientific research, an incubator for creativity, and a stimulating environment for learning and excellence.” The university has achieved important achievements in the field of education and scientific research, as it has received many local and international awards, and has published many scientific researches in refereed scientific journals.

- Tabuk University is keen to provide a distinguished educational and research environment for its students, by providing modern educational and research facilities, providing distinguished educational programs, and providing student support and care.

Informaion about Tabuk University :

  1. Area: The total area of Tabuk University is about 100 million square meters.

  2. Students: The number of male and female students at the University of Tabuk is about 50,000 students.

  3. Colleges and Programs: The University of Tabuk has 18 colleges and institutes, offering more than 100 educational programs in various scientific and humanitarian disciplines.

  4. Scientific research: The University of Tabuk has received many local and international awards in the field of scientific research.

Among the achievements of the University of Tabuk :

  1. Obtaining academic accreditation from the Education and Training Evaluation Commission for its various educational programs.

  2. Obtaining local and international awards in the field of education and scientific research.

  3. Many scientific papers have been published in refereed scientific journals.
